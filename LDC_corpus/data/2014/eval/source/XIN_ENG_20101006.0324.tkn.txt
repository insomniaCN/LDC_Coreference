<HEADLINE> 
Publicis buys 49 percent stake in Brazilian ad agency 
< / HEADLINE> 
<DATELINE> 
PARIS , Oct . 6 ( Xinhua ) 
< / DATELINE> 
<TEXT> 
<P> 
Communications giant Publicis Groupe said Wednesday it has purchased 49 percent 
of Brazil 's leading advertising agency .
< / P> 
<P> 
The French firm signed an agreement to acquire a 49 - percent stake in the Talent 
Group , one of the most prominent advertising groups in Brazil , Publicis said .
< / P> 
<P> 
" This transaction is in line with Publicis Groupe 's strategy of targeted 
acquisitions aimed at expanding its leadership in high - growth markets such as 
Brazil , " the company said .
< / P> 
<P> 
The deal was estimated to cost Publicis around $ 110 million U.S. dollars , a 
person close to the group told Dow Jones Newswire .
< / P> 
<P> 
The Talent Group acquisition was the company 's third in Brazil this year . It 
earlier purchased the digital agency AG2 and a minority stake in the Taterka 
advertising agency .
< / P> 
<P> 
Headquartered in Sao Paulo , the Talent Group is one of Brazil 's leading 
advertising firms , covering a wide range of advertising services including 
strategy , creative , media planning and digital communication .
< / P> 
<P> 
Publicis Groupe , which operates in 104 countries and employs 1,000 workers in 
Brazil , generates half of its income from digital businesses in emerging markets 
with high growth potential .
< / P> 
<P> 
That proportion was expected to reach 60 percent over the coming three months .
< / P> 
<P> 
During the first half of this year , Publicis Groupe 's net income reached 213 
million euros ( 294.4 million U.S. dollars ) , rising 27.5 percent over the same 
period last year .
< / P>
