<HEADLINE> 
Blackhawks ' Kane set to appear in Buffalo court 
< / HEADLINE> 
<DATELINE> 
BUFFALO , New York 2009-08-20 16:03:51 UTC 
< / DATELINE> 
<TEXT> 
<P> 
Chicago Blackhawks star forward Patrick Kane is scheduled to appear in Buffalo city court for arraignment on misdemeanor charges that he beat up a cab driver over a fare dispute .
< / P> 
<P> 
Kane 's appearance before judge Thomas Amodeo on Thursday comes a day after a grand jury indicted the player on misdemeanor assault and theft charges . The grand jury dismissed a more serious felony charge of second - degree robbery .
< / P> 
<P> 
Kane and his cousin , James Kane , were arrested Aug . 9 after cabbie Jan Radecki told police they attacked him when he said he did n't have 20 cents in change for the fare . James Kane was indicted on the same charges and is also due in court .
< / P> 
<P> 
The cab driver had bruises and broken glasses .
< / P>
