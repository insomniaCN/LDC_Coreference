<HEADLINE> 
Romania pres nominates a Bucharest mayor as PM 
< / HEADLINE> 
<DATELINE> 
BUCHAREST , Romania 2009-11-06 10:23:08 UTC 
< / DATELINE> 
<TEXT> 
<P> 
Romania 's president has nominated a Bucharest district mayor as the country 's new prime minister .
< / P> 
<P> 
Liviu Negoita , who lead 's the capital 's sector 3 , said Friday he will form a team in three days to present to parliament . He says he wants a government with broad political support .
< / P> 
<P> 
Negoita belongs to the Democratic Liberal Party , which President Traian Basescu used to lead .
< / P> 
<P> 
It was not clear whether he had enough support to be confirmed . This week lawmakers rejected a Basescu - nominated government led by finance adviser Lucian Croitoru .
< / P> 
<P> 
Romania has been without a proper government since Oct . 13 when parliament dismissed Prime Minister Emil Boc in a no confidence vote .
< / P>
