<HEADLINE> 
Convicted bomb plotter seeks release in UK 
< / HEADLINE> 
<DATELINE> 
LONDON 2010-11-25 13:34:25 UTC 
< / DATELINE> 
<TEXT> 
<P> 
A Jordanian man convicted in 1986 of attempting to blow up an Israeli airliner is asking a British court to order his release from prison .
< / P> 
<P> 
Nezar Hindawi was convicted of packing explosives in the luggage of his pregnant Irish fiancee , who was traveling to Tel Aviv on El - Al . She was unaware of his plot , which was detected before the flight departed .
< / P> 
<P> 
Hindawi was sentenced to 45 years in prison , said to the be the longest sentence ever set by an English court . He became eligible for parole in 2001 , but the government rejected a subsequent recommendation from the Parole Board that Hindawi be released .
< / P> 
<P> 
The hearing on his case began Thursday and is expected to continue through Friday .
< / P>
