<HEADLINE> 
SHOOTING REPORTED AT WASHINGTON NAVY YARD 
< / HEADLINE> 
<TEXT> 
<P> 
This article is part of TIMES EXPRESS . It is a condensed version of a story that will appear in tomorrow ’s New York Times . ) ; 
< / P> 
<P> 
WASHINGTON - A shooting at a naval headquarters here on Monday left at least four people injured , including one police officer , the police said .
< / P> 
<P> 
The officer , whose condition was unknown , was taken to a hospital . The gunman was barricaded , the police said .
< / P> 
<P> 
An individual fired three shots at 8:20 a.m. at the Washington Navy Yard , in the southeastern part of the city , the Navy said on Twitter . Workers were told to shelter in place , and emergency personnel were on the scene .
< / P> 
<P> 
The shooting occurred at the Naval Sea Systems Command ’s headquarters , where about 3,000 service members , civilians and contractors work on the Navy ’s fleet , the Navy said in a statement .
