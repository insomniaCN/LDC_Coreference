FALLEN - INVESTOR - COMEBACK ( Undated ) – Fred Eckert , a onetime Goldman Sachs partner who soared as a star in “ vulture ” investing in ailing companies , saw his fortunes fall in the turmoil of the financial crisis . HESS - BOARD ( Houston ) – Few boardroom battles are resolved like that over Hess Corp . After marathon talks ending only hours before shareholders began arriving for the oil company ’s annual investor meeting , Hess agreed to give the activist hedge fund Elliott Management three board seats , in exchange for winning the hedge fund ’s support of the company ’s own slate of five directors . The pact ends one of the biggest corporate governance fights of the year , as activist investors grow increasingly bolder in seeking out ever - larger targets . By Michael J . de la Merced .
