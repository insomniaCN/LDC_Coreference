<HEADLINE>
Indonesian to hang over Malaysia wedding shooting
</HEADLINE>
<DATELINE>
KUALA LUMPUR, Malaysia 2010-05-08 03:24:50 UTC
</DATELINE>
<TEXT>
<P>
A Malaysian court has sentenced an Indonesian man to death for fatally shooting five people during a wedding reception on remote Borneo island.
</P>
<P>
The national news agency Bernama reported Saturday that the court in Sarawak state sentenced Nyambang Entuhan on Friday to death by hanging over the 2007 shooting.
</P>
<P>
The man, who is married to a local woman, reportedly became angry and opened fire after the village chief warned wedding guests not to marry Indonesians. The bride and groom survived.
</P>
<P>
The report said he was also sentenced to 22 years in prison for wounding nine others and illegal firearm possession. His lawyer has said he would appeal.
</P>
<P>
Officials could not immediately be reached for comment Saturday.
</P>
